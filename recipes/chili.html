Chili

The Best Chili in Michigan

This chili recipe has been in the family for decades. Every year at christmas Grandma will bring a pot. One is never enough!.

The combination of spice and sweet make this dish perfect for anyone a fan of chili.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1 jalepeno
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 2T chili powder
  • 1t cumin
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 14oz crushed tomatoes
  • 19oz kidney beans
  • 14 oz diced tomatoes
  • 1.5 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup beer
  • 1T tomato paste
  • 1T brown sugar
  • salt and pepper to taste
